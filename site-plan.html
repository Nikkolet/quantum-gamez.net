Overview

Did you know?

The word "quantum" has Latin roots and means "how much."

Purpose

My idea is to create a fun and engaging website for all ages to learn Quantum Physics. It will teach them its history, fun facts, basic concepts, and fun games for them to enjoy.

There is a high demand for more advanced technology to meet humanity's needs and children are our future leaders. I am a web developer who can create this website collaborating with credible and qualified resources.

What if the world's technology could help cure all diseases, make space travel as common as traveling in a car, or expand the limits of virtual reality so that everyone can view history simulations as if they stepped back in time?

The people who will create that kind of technology tomorrow are our children today. We cannot invest in our future without investing in our children.

It's for all of us.

Audience

My audience is children and young adults interested in learning about the world around them at their own pace. Quantum Gamez is for even the most complete beginner in the world of quantum physics.

Young Adults will love our high-graphic games and articles, and children can enjoy our variety of games, coloring pages, and videos.

Parents can breathe easy knowing their child or young adult is having fun learning on a website safe for them to explore.
